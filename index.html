About Our Campus

Finding your way around a large university campus can be confusing, especially for new students or visitors. Manipal UniNav is our interactive navigation platform designed to make this easier. It helps students quickly locate classrooms, labs, libraries, cafeterias, hostels, sports facilities, and other key spots around the campus. With a simple search or click, users can view the shortest route, estimated walking time, and important landmarks along the way—so no more wandering aimlessly or arriving late.
