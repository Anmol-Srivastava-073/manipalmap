About Manipal UniNav

Finding your way around a large university campus can be confusing, especially for new students or visitors. Manipal UniNav is our interactive navigation platform designed to make this easier. Manipal UniNav is built with a simple vision: to help everyone on campus find their way without hassle—whether it's a new student figuring out where their next class is, or a visitor locating the auditorium. With open-source tools and thoughtful design, we’ve created a solution that’s light, fast, and scalable. The project is split into two core parts, each maintained in separate repositories and deployed individually:

The Interactive Map Module – This is the heart of the navigation experience. It uses maps sourced from OpenStreetMap and other open-source map providers, ensuring free and reliable access to detailed, customizable mapping data. For real-time routing and pathfinding, we’ve integrated the OpenRouteService (ORS) API, which provides accurate walking directions and seamless navigation.

We've divided the university into two distinct zones:

The College Campus – housing academic blocks, libraries, labs, and administrative buildings. The Hostel Campus – including student housing, mess areas, and nearby recreational spots. This separation helps in organizing routes more efficiently and ensures users can easily toggle between academic and residential navigation.

HOW UNINAV WORKS

Step 1: Open the Website

Go to mujmaps.netlify.app on your browser. The homepage will load with the interactive MUJ campus map.

Step 2: Search Your Location

Use the search bar to type the name of a building, block, or facility (e.g., “Library”, “Hostel”, “Mess”).

Step 3: Get Directions

Click a result to view details and tap “Directions”. The route will be highlighted on the map.

Step 4: Navigate the Campus

Follow the highlighted path to reach your destination within the MUJ campus.
